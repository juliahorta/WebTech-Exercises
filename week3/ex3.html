London


London is the capital and largest city of the United Kingdon.

It is widely considered the world's leading global city - especially as it has a diverse range of people and cultrues, with more than 300 languages spoken in the region

The first major settlement was founded by the Romans after the invasion of 43 AD, however the legendary Queen Boudica stormed it, burning it to the ground.

Upon the collapse of the Roman rule, London ceased to be a capital after having been effectively abandonded.

The Anglo-Saxon and Viking period then began bringing London to become beyond all comparison the largest town in England by the 11th Century.

In 1300, the city was still confined within the Roman walls.
